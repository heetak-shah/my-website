I am currently a predoctoral research fellow in Economics and Operations, Information, and Technology at Stanford Graduate School of Business. I work with Modibo Camara on understanding methods to elicit preferences and on the welfare impact of the metrics used for evaluating recommender systems, and with Dan Iancu and Omer Karaduman on understanding cannibalization of customers when a firm opens a new store near an existing store.

Previously, I graduated (B.Tech. + M.Tech.) from Indian Institute of Technology Bombay, where I worked with Prof. T. T. Niranjan and K. S. Mallikarjuna Rao.
